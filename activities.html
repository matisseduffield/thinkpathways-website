Daily Life & Goals

Support in Action

We focus on practical, enjoyable tasks that build independence. Here is a snapshot of the activities we can facilitate to help you achieve your goals.

Life Skills Nutrition

Mastering the Kitchen

From planning a menu and shopping for ingredients to cooking a healthy meal. We support you to navigate the kitchen safely and confidently.

Home-Based Support
Independence

Travel Training

Building the confidence to get from A to B. We assist with reading timetables, using MetroCards, and navigating public transport routes safely.

Adelaide Metro
Social

Community Bowling

Connecting with peers and enjoying friendly competition in a supported environment.

Budgeting

Market Shopping

Practicing money handling, social interaction, and choosing fresh produce.

Health

Active Living

Swimming, gym sessions, or gentle walks to maintain physical health and wellbeing.

Creative

Art & Expression

Exploring creativity through painting, drawing, or crafts. A great way to express emotions and build fine motor skills.

Empowering individuals with disabilities to live life their way.
