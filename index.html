Our Services

Supporting your independence

We offer a comprehensive range of disability support services tailored to your NDIS plan and personal goals.

In-Home Support

Assistance with daily living tasks, personal care, and household management to help you maintain your independence at home.

Community Access

Support to participate in community activities, social groups, appointments, and recreational events.

Skill Development

Mentoring and training to build life skills such as cooking, budgeting, travel training, and technology use.

Transport Assistance

Safe and reliable transport to get you to your appointments, work, or social engagements on time.

Complex Care

Specialized support for participants with high intensity daily personal activities or complex health needs.

Plan Management

Assistance with managing your NDIS funding, paying invoices, and tracking your budget expenditure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and NDIS support.

Are you NDIS registered?

We are an NDIS compatible provider. We can support participants who are Plan Managed or Self Managed.

What areas do you service?

We currently provide support services across the Greater Metropolitan area. Please contact us to check if we have a worker in your specific suburb.

Is there a waiting list?

We currently have capacity to take on new participants. Once we understand your needs, we can usually start services within 1-2 weeks.
