Transparent & Fair

Simple, NDIS-Compatible Pricing

We believe in full transparency. Think Pathways strictly adheres to the NDIS Pricing Arrangements and Price Limits. You will never be charged above the official rate.

Standard Support Rates

Based on the 'Assistance with Self-Care Activities - Standard' line item.

Time / Day Description Rate (Hourly)
Weekday Daytime Standard support between 6am - 8pm $67.56
Weekday Evening Support finishing after 8pm $74.56

Sunday All day Sunday $122.51
Public Holiday Recognized SA Public Holidays $149.88
Verify current rates on NDIS.gov.au

Provider Travel

We charge for the time it takes a worker to travel to you, capped at 30 minutes within the metropolitan area.

Standard Labour Rate

Participant Transport

If we drive you during your shift (e.g. to the shops), a per-kilometer rate applies to cover vehicle costs.

Establishment Fee

A one-off fee may apply for new participants who have at least 20 hours of support in their plan.

Temporary Transformation Payment (TTP)

Think Pathways is compliant with the TTP requirements set out by the NDIA and charges the TTP rate for applicable services. This allows us to maintain the highest quality of staff and training.
